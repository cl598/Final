Calypso

Calypso is part of the maintenance department led by Hau. She is in charge of maintaining library and keeping everything up to date.

Appearance

Dark-skinned woman with purple hair tied in a bun. Often seen in a dark purple sweater vest and black midi skirt.

Personality

Quiet and calm.

Abilities

 Search magic

Focuses magic energy into her eyes to browse through the library.
