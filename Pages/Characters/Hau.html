Hau

Hau is the guild manager, and one of the guild masters of "Keepers of the Dark." Maintains the mundane departments like finance, supplies, etc.

Appearance

An above average height woman with long, white hair. Often seen with a kimono with jewelry.

Personality

Elegant and gentle.

Abilities

 Eternal magic

Manipulates the magical energies and turn them into different forms at command.
