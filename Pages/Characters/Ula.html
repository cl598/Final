Ula

Ula is the defense manager, and one of the guild masters of "Keepers of the Dark." Maintains the defense department of the guild, and makes sure the barriers stay in tact.

Appearance

Small-sized woman. Often seen in overalls, or any fashionable outfits.

Personality

Straightforward and upbeat.

Abilities

 Shield magic

Can summon any sized wall with magical energy to repel or absorb attacks.



Alias  "Toad"

Age  18 years old

Eye color  Cyan

Hair color  Beige

Height  5'2

Position Defense
