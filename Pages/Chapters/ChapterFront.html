Prologue


Magic, is that real? Absolutely! Magic has been existing since the birth of mankind, and has been evolving with the creative minds from the first generation and forward. Alongside magic, science has been advancing with the people born with no magic energy. With the combination of magic and science, a new modern era has emerged generations later.

To create order in this new world, guilds are made. Guilds are associations featuring certain skill sets, which helps differentiate who is the best at what. Guilds come in many different forms; carpentry, metalwork, monster hunting, etc. are the essentials to maintain society.

One guild stood apart from the many that are created, and that one is called the “Keepers of the Dark.” It is known as the most powerful guild in this current generation, with many different forms of talent that join together in that single area.

And the one guild that will face the most danger.
