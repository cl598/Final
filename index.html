Synopsis


The smell of blood is foul.

The sight of blood is horrifying.

Everything about blood is terrible, even if spilling it is supposed to be for the greater good.

A white haired figure looked down at the large, body filled field from a small cliff above. “Why did it have to come to this…” she sighed. She began grabbing pieces of her shattering and ripped armor apart and let it drop to the ground, standing bare with her freshly cut scars. “I have to get them back.” She knelt down, touching her forehead to activate her third eye. “I have to… no matter what.”
